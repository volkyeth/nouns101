title: Executing Malicious Transactions

If you interact with a malicious contract, your wallet is as good as <Nutshell children="drained" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/drained.mdx" />.

Always be wary of requests which require you to pay <Nutshell children="gas fees" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/gas_fee.mdx" /> to execute them.
