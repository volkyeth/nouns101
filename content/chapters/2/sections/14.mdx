---
title: Protecting Your Digital Assets
---

To protect against these and other risks, it is important for you to take appropriate security measures. These include:
- Using strong and unique passwords
- Enabling two-factor authentication
- Avoiding suspicious links or messages (don’t even click!)
- Keeping  software and applications up to date
- Know the risks & stay vigilant when using web3 technologies
- Using a [[Hardware Wallet]].
- Using a “Hot Wallet” to mint or interact with contracts.
