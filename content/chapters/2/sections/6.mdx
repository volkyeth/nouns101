title: Sending NFTs
---

To send an NFT, you need the recipient's wallet address, and some crypto to pay gas fees.

For some digital wallets (like Metamask), you can only see your NFT via their mobile app.

If you're on a desktop computer you should find a <Nutshell children="third-party" body={<>Unfortunately, some crypto wallets do not have an option to send NFTs via their desktop version.      You can send your NFTs with the help of NFT Marketplaces like OpenSea.      Alternatively, you can get a wallet which has this feature.</>} /> platform or \[\[marketplace]] to transfer your NFTs.

On Opensea, you simply choose the respective NFT you’d like to send, and hit “Transfer.”

Next, you’ll be prompted to input a wallet address, and then sign a transaction to initiate the \[\[transfer]] on the blockchain.
