The web3 space is notorious for hacks and scams.

But staying safe in Web3 is simple: Don't give scammers access to your wallet!

**Private Keys**
NEVER reveal your private keys online or even IRL. Same goes for your [[seed phrase]].

**Executing Transactions**
If you interact with a malicious contract, your wallet is as good as drained.

Always be wary of requests which require you to pay [[gas fees]] to execute them.

However, signing a [[signature request]] poses no risk.

**Buying A Hardware Wallet**
Most beginners start out with software wallets, which are free.

If you have a decent amount of money in crypto, you should consider getting a [[hardware wallet]].
