---
title: WTF Is An NFT?
---



NFT stands for [[Non Fungible Token]]. Was that any clearer? Haha!

NFTs are essentially digital tokens on the blockchain, but they are one-of-a-kind.

![](/assets/7.jpg)

Even if two NFTs look exactly the same, cryptographically, they are different. Think of this like a serial number identifying and differentiating two cars.
