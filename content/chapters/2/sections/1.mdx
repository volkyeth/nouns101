title: WTF Is An NFT?
---

NFT stands for <Nutshell children="Non Fungible Token" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/nft.mdx" />. Was that any clearer? Haha!

NFTs are essentially digital tokens on the <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/blockchain.mdx" children="blockchain" />, but they are one-of-a-kind.

![](/assets/7.jpg)

Even if two NFTs look exactly the same, cryptographically, they are different. Think of this like a serial number identifying and differentiating two cars.
