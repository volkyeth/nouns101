title: Staying Safe In Web3
---

The web3 space is notorious for hacks and scams.

Staying safe in Web3 is simple: **Don't give scammers access to your wallet!**

Granting access by interacting with a malicious smart contract or revealing your private key (aka, your <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/seed_phrase.mdx" children="seed phrase" /> ) are both ways to immediately having your digital assets <Nutshell children="drained" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/drained.mdx" /> from your wallet.

On the following pages we'll go over some common ways your digital wallet can become compromised.&#x20;
