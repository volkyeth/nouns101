---
title: Staying Safe In Web3
---

The web3 space is notorious for hacks and scams. 
 
Granting access by signing a [[malicious contract]], or revealing your private key or seed phrase are a one way ticket to having your digital assets [[drained]]. 
 
Staying safe in Web3 is simple: **Don't give scammers access to your wallet!**
