---
title: Where Can I Get Started in Nouns DAO?
---

Want to get involved in the Nouns ecosystem? Head over to our website, check out our Discourse (Idea Board), and play around with Noun accessories in our Playground!

Join our Discord and have a vibe check - on what’s “Nounish”. Feel free to ask any questions about Nouns DAO in the appropriate channels!

Nouns DAO loves builders - propose ideas in Discord, Disco urse, and if the community loves it, take that idea to the next level on Prop House or even on-chain!
