Despite being around for little over a year, Nouns DAO has built quite a number of impressive projects.

For a start, we have built ‘Prop House’, a place for ANYONE with an Ethereum wallet to propose ideas - with the chance of getting funded.

This has not only impacted the Nouns community, but also branched out to other communities such as OCM, Mfers and Cryptoadz.

On top of that, Nouns DAO has commissioned a comic book series, produced luxury sunglasses, and even named a newly discovered species of frog, “Noun”!
