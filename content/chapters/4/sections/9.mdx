---
title: How Can I Contribute to Lil Nouns DAO?
---

Lil Nouns DAO, and the Nounish ecosystem believe in consistently upgrading our infrastructure, continuous proliferation, and growing our unique culture.
 
**All quality ideas are welcome;** whether it be art, music, governance infrastructure, IRL projects, fun experiments, etc.
 
Ultimately, the DAO decides if we should want your idea to come to life.
