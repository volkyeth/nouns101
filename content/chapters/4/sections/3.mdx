Of course, Nouns DAO is open to its holders, also called ‘Nouners’. Although Nouners have certain privileges such as proposing ideas directly on-chain, access to private chat groups and calls… they’re not the only ones who can participate!

Nouns DAO is open to everyone, with various avenues for builders to propose ideas, join in the discussion, and even govern the DAO!
