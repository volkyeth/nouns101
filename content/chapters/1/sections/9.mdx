There are many types of software wallets (aka <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/hot_wallet.mdx" children="Hot Wallets" /> ), and it’s important to do research to determine which is best suited for you.

For the purposes of this course, we’re suggesting you set up an Ethereum wallet through <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/metamask.mdx" children="MetaMask" />. Please take your time creating your digital wallet now. This should take approximately 5-10 minutes.

Visit [https://metamask.io/](https://metamask.io/) or find metamask in the app store to get started. Simply follow the on screen steps, or watch this video for a walk through before or as you get started:

<YouTube youTubeId="Af_lQ1zUnoM" />

**Note:** It is very very important you NEVER share the seed phrase you create when setting up your wallet. **We repeat — NEVER**.
