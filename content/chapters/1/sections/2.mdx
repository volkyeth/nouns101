Blockchain is the backbone of <Nutshell children="Web3" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/web3.mdx" />. Put simply, it is a database.

Without it, cryptocurrency, NFTs, and DAOs would not exist (if you don’t know what these are yet, don’t worry! We’ll get there).

More technically, the blockchain is a <Nutshell children="database" body={<>A database is a collection of data that is organized and stored in a specific way, allowing it to be accessed, modified, and analyzed efficiently.      \   Databases are used to store and manage large amounts of structured and unstructured data, such as customer information, financial records, and inventory data.</>} />



&#x20;and houses a ledger (or record) of transactions.
