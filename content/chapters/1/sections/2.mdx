title: Understanding The Blockchain
---

Blockchain is the backbone of <Nutshell children="Web3" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/web3.mdx" />. Put simply, it is a database.

![](/assets../../../../1.jpg)

Without it, cryptocurrency, NFTs, and DAOs would not exist (if you don’t know what these are yet, don’t worry! We’ll get there).

More technically, the blockchain is a \[\[database]] and houses a ledger (or record) of transactions.
