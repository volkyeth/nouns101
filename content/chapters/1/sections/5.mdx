title: Cryptocurrency De-mystified
---

Cryptocurrency is an :intangible:, digital currency on the blockchain.

This means that crypto is not run by governments or banks. It’s run decentrally.

![](/assets/3.jpg)

Cryptocurrency relies on \[\[blockchain]] technology to be secured, transferred, and created.

Without the blockchain, crypto would not exist. For that reason, cryptocurrency is a <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/peer_to_peer.mdx" children="peer-to-peer (P2P)" /> system.

Cryptocurrency is \[\[stored]] on the blockchain, accessed via crypto wallets, and can be bought with <Nutshell glossaryEntry="content/glossary/fiat.mdx" children="FIAT" />\[\[FIAT]] on \[\[crypto exchanges]].

There are many kinds of cryptocurrencies, residing on different blockchains. \[\[Bitcoin]], \[\[Ethereum]], and \[\[Solana]] are three popular crypto \[\[tokens]].

Some popular crypto \[\[exchanges]] include \[\[Coinbase]], \[\[Binance]], and \[\[Kraken]], among many others.

Before signing up for an exchange, it’s important to \[\[DYOR]].<Nutshell />
