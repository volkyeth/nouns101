As explained in the previous section, there are different types of wallets.&#x20;

After purchasing your crypto on an exchange, we suggest you *store* your crypto long term on a non-custodial wallet. Doing so allows you to secure your crypto on the <Nutshell children="blockchain" glossaryEntry="content/glossary/blockchain.mdx" />, and not in an exchange.&#x20;

![](/assets/bit-crypto-wallet.jpg)

Contrary to popular belief, crypto wallets do not store your crypto. They simply allow you to access your crypto.
