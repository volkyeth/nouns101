Cryptocurrency is an <Nutshell term="intangible"><Intangible/></Nutshell>, digital currency on the blockchain.

This means that crypto is not run by governments or banks. Crypto is run decentrally.

**The Details**
Cryptocurrency relies on blockchain technology to be secured, transferred, and created.

Without the blockchain, crypto would not exist.

For that reason, cryptocurrency is a [[Peer to peer:peer-to-peer (P2P) system]].

Cryptocurrency is <Nutshell term="stored in digital wallets"><Stored/></Nutshell>, and can be bought with [[FIAT]] on crypto exchanges.
