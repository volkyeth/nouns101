A cryptocurrency exchange is a platform that allows users to buy, sell, and trade cryptocurrencies. These exchanges typically provide a secure and user-friendly interface that enables users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies using various payment methods, such as bank transfers, credit or debit cards, or other digital currencies.
