[Discord](https://discord.com/) is a popular communication platform that is often used by online communities, including communities of people who are interested in blockchain technology and NFTs.

In a NFT Discord server, members may discuss various topics related to NFTs, such as buying and selling NFTs, creating NFTs, and using NFTs in various applications. They may also share news and updates about the NFT market and various NFT projects, and may provide support and guidance to each other as they explore the world of NFTs.
