A term coined by the NounsDAO community for projects and initiatives funded by the NounsDAO.

The term is open to interpretation, but was defined by one of the founders of NounsDAO, @punk4156 on Twitter as:

"do good with no [expectation](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=expectation) of return, create positive externalities, embrace [absurdity](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=absurdity) & difference, teach people about nouns & [crypto](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=crypto), have fun"
