An airdrop is the process of distributing tokens or cryptocurrencies to users for free. Airdrops are often used by blockchain projects as a way to promote their project and increase awareness among potential users.&#x20;

Airdrops can also be used to distribute tokens to existing users as a reward for their support or participation in the project. They are typically distributed to users who hold a specific cryptocurrency, or have interacted with the project in some way.&#x20;

Airdropped tokens are typically received by users in their cryptocurrency wallet, and can be traded or used on the project's platform.
