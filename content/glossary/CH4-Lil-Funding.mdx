Lil Funding is a grants program through Lil Nouns to help fund builders and core contributors more quickly than on-chain proposals. Lil Funding requests are typically under 10E. Lil Funding is filled via On-chain proposals that send ETH to a multi-sig controlled and managed by the Lil Funding signers.

To apply for lil funding, visit [lilfunding.wtf](lilfunding.wtf).

For a list of Lil Funding projects visit [Lil Proposals](https://lilproposals.notion.site/Lil-Proposals-5290faa7a14a444c8e8a96db0d41d2a9).&#x20;
