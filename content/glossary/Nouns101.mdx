## The Project

Nouns101.wtf is a web3 onboarding platform sponsored by the Lil Nouns DAO through on-chain Proposal #57. Here, users can learn about web3 by forming a basic understanding of the blockchain, NFTs and DAOs. Readers can learn how to create a crypto wallet and have access to claim a free NFT (a bitNoun) when ready. Readers will also learn about the Nouns DAO ecosystem, and more about Lil Nouns DAO and how to join themselves.

Because the world of web3 and  is vast and expanding day-by-day, we encourage you to do your own research, stay safe, and never share confidential information with anyone.&#x20;
